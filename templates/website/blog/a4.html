Airport Taxi Etiquette: What Every Traveler Should Know

1. Tipping
It’s customary to tip airport taxi drivers, especially if they assist with luggage or provide exceptional service. A tip of 10-15% of the fare is generally appreciated.

2. Communicate Clearly
Make sure to communicate your destination and any specific requests clearly. This avoids confusion and ensures a smoother journey.

3. Respect the Vehicle
Treat the taxi with respect. Avoid eating or drinking inside the vehicle unless permitted by the driver.

4. Safety First
Always buckle up, even in taxis. Safety is paramount, and wearing a seatbelt is mandatory in most countries.

5. Be Ready for Pickup
Be punctual for your taxi pickup, especially for airport transfers, as drivers often work on tight schedules.

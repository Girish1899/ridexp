How to Spot and Avoid Common Airport Taxi Scams

1. Overcharging:
Always agree on a fare beforehand or make sure the meter is running to avoid being overcharged by taxi drivers who may inflate rates.

2. Rigged Meters:
Some taxis use rigged meters that run faster than normal, increasing the fare. Use licensed airport taxi services to avoid this scam.

3. Fake Taxis:
Watch out for unlicensed taxis that offer rides outside of designated taxi areas. Always use authorized taxi services from the airport.

4. Route Diversions:
Drivers may take longer routes to inflate the fare. If you are unfamiliar with the city, use GPS on your phone to ensure the shortest route.

5. Demanding Extra Fees:
Some taxi drivers may demand extra fees for luggage or other services. Make sure all fees are clear before starting your ride.

Affordable Airport Taxi Services for Large Groups

1. Book Minivans or Buses:
Many airport taxi services offer larger vehicles like minivans or buses that can accommodate more passengers and luggage, making it more affordable for large groups.

2. Share the Cost:
Splitting the cost of a taxi among a large group can make the fare per person significantly cheaper compared to individual rides.

3. Pre-Book Group Transfers:
Pre-booking a group airport transfer can often secure a lower rate and ensure the right vehicle is available for your group’s needs.

4. Look for Group Discounts:
Some airport taxi services offer group discounts, so be sure to inquire when booking.

5. Check for Luggage Capacity:
Ensure the vehicle you book has enough space for everyone’s luggage, as this could require booking a larger vehicle, but often the larger the group, the lower the cost per person.

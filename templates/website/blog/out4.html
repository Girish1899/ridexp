Essential Tips for Safe Outstation Travel by Taxi

1. Verify Driver Identity:
Before getting into the vehicle, always verify the driver's identity. Ensure the driver matches the information provided by the taxi service, including their name and photo ID. This is a critical step for your safety.

2. Share Your Itinerary:
Sharing your travel itinerary with a trusted family member or friend can add an extra layer of safety. Let them know your expected departure and arrival times, along with any stops along the way.

3. Track Your Route:
Use a navigation app to follow the route taken by the driver during your journey. This ensures the driver is following the correct and agreed path and helps you stay on track in unfamiliar areas.

4. Emergency Contacts:
Keep a list of emergency contacts easily accessible on your phone. This can be crucial if you need assistance during the trip, whether for a health emergency or other concerns.

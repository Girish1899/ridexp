Description

Join us on a serene and enlightening religious tour from Bangalore to Shravanabelagola, a significant pilgrimage site for followers of Jainism. Visit the iconic 57-foot Gomateshwara statue, also known as Bahubali, one of the largest monolithic statues in the world. Shravanabelagola is a town filled with spiritual history, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking a deeper understanding of Jain culture and heritage.

Top Attractions in Shravanabelagola

  • Gomateshwara Statue: A massive monolithic statue of Bahubali, symbolizing peace and non-violence in Jainism.
  • Vindhyagiri Hill: Climb over 600 steps to reach the statue and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Chandragiri Hill: A smaller hill with ancient Jain temples dating back to the 2nd century BCE.
  • Jain Temples: Explore beautifully constructed Jain temples, rich in history and religious significance.

Why Visit Shravanabelagola?

Shravanabelagola is one of the most important religious sites in India, known for its Jain temples and the iconic Bahubali statue. It offers visitors a unique opportunity to connect with the profound spiritual teachings of Jainism and explore centuries-old temples. Whether you're a pilgrim or a traveler interested in ancient history, Shravanabelagola is a destination that provides peace and spiritual insight.

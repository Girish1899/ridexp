Description

Embark on a journey to Agumbe, known as the "Cherrapunji of the South," and explore the biodiversity-rich rainforests of the Western Ghats. This package includes visits to scenic waterfalls, lush green forests, and trekking trails, ideal for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Top Attractions in Agumbe

  • Agumbe Rainforest: Trek through lush green rainforests, home to a wide range of flora and fauna.
  • Jogigundi Falls: A serene waterfall nestled deep within the forest, perfect for a peaceful retreat.
  • Kundadri Hill: A scenic hilltop known for its panoramic views and a beautiful Jain temple.
  • Sunset Viewpoint: Experience breathtaking sunsets over the Western Ghats.
  • Onake Abbi Falls: A hidden gem, perfect for those looking to explore offbeat locations.

Why Visit Agumbe?

Agumbe is a must-visit destination for those who love nature and adventure. With its rich biodiversity, serene trekking trails, and stunning waterfalls, Agumbe offers a peaceful retreat and a chance to reconnect with nature.

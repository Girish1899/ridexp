Description

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a treasure trove of historical wonders. Our Bangalore to Hampi heritage tour package offers a deep dive into the ancient Vijayanagar Empire, with visits to iconic temples, monuments, and stone sculptures.

Top Attractions in Hampi

  • Virupaksha Temple: One of the oldest and most significant temples in Hampi, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The intricate carvings and historical importance make it a must-visit.
  • Vittala Temple Complex: Famous for its iconic stone chariot and the musical pillars, this complex represents the architectural brilliance of the Vijayanagara Empire.
  • Lotus Mahal: A beautiful structure blending Islamic and Hindu architectural styles. It served as a recreational palace for the royal women.

Why Visit Hampi?

Hampi was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is now a UNESCO World Heritage site. The ruins offer a glimpse into India's rich history and heritage.

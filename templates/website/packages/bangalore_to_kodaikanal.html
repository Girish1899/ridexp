Description

Experience the beauty of Kodaikanal, known as the Princess of Hill Stations. This package includes visits to Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker's Walk, and Bryant Park, making it a perfect trip for nature enthusiasts and peace seekers.

Top Attractions in Kodaikanal

  • Kodaikanal Lake: Boating amidst scenic beauty.
  • Coaker's Walk: A picturesque walkway for nature lovers.
  • Bryant Park: A beautifully maintained garden for relaxation.
  • Dolphin's Nose: A breathtaking viewpoint.
  • Pine Forest: Explore the serene pine woods.
  • Kurinji Andavar Temple: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. It's also renowned for the Kurinji flowers, which bloom once every 12 years.

Why Visit Kodaikanal?

Kodaikanal offers a peaceful escape from the hustle of city life. The cool climate, lush greenery, and beautiful landscapes make it a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those in need of a serene retreat.

Description

Known as the "Scotland of India," Coorg offers lush green landscapes, rolling hills, and tranquil coffee plantations. Our Bangalore to Coorg travel package is designed for nature enthusiasts who are looking for a refreshing break from city life.

Top Attractions in Coorg

  • Abbey Falls: A picturesque waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Raja’s Seat: A stunning viewpoint for sunsets and valley views.
  • Dubare Elephant Camp: An elephant training camp offering interaction with the elephants.
  • Coffee Plantations: Take a guided tour of Coorg’s famous coffee estates.
  • Nagarhole National Park: A wildlife sanctuary home to tigers, elephants, and other exotic animals.

Why Visit Coorg?

Coorg is a paradise for nature lovers, offering a blend of adventure, tranquility, and scenic beauty. Visitors can enjoy serene coffee plantations, beautiful waterfalls, and mesmerizing views, making Coorg a perfect weekend getaway.

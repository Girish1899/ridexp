Top Attractions in Nandi Hills

  • Tipu’s Drop: This 600-meter-high cliff offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and a peek into history, as it was a famous execution site during Tipu Sultan’s reign.
  • Nandi Temple: Located at the base of the hill, this ancient temple dedicated to Nandi (Lord Shiva's bull) showcases impressive stone carvings and spiritual significance.
  • Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace: A historically significant palace that offers a glimpse into the opulence of the Mysore ruler's architectural style, surrounded by well-maintained gardens.
  • Amrita Sarovar: Known as the "Lake of Nectar," this perennial lake provides serene surroundings for a peaceful retreat, making it a beautiful spot for nature lovers.

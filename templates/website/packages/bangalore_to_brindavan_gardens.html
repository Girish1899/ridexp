Description

Discover the royal heritage of Mysore and the captivating beauty of Brindavan Gardens in this specially curated travel package. Mysore, known for its rich cultural history, is home to the famous Mysore Palace, while Brindavan Gardens offers a mesmerizing light and fountain show in the evenings.

Top Attractions in Mysore & Brindavan Gardens

  • Mysore Palace: Explore the grandeur of the Wodeyar dynasty's palace.
  • Chamundi Hill: Visit the famous Chamundeshwari Temple on the hilltop.
  • St. Philomena's Church: Admire the Gothic architecture of this historical church.
  • Brindavan Gardens: Witness the stunning musical fountain and light show.
  • Mysore Zoo: One of the oldest and well-maintained zoos in India.
  • Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary: A paradise for bird watchers located near Mysore.

Why Visit Mysore and Brindavan Gardens?

Mysore is a perfect blend of history, culture, and art. From the majestic Mysore Palace to the serene Chamundi Hills, the city is a haven for heritage lovers. The nearby Brindavan Gardens, with its colorful fountains and light show, offers a spectacular end to your day trip.

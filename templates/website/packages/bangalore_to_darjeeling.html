Description

Embark on a journey to Darjeeling, the Queen of Hills, with our Bangalore to Darjeeling Hill Station Tour. Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Darjeeling offers stunning views of snow-capped peaks, lush tea gardens, and vibrant Tibetan culture. This tour includes visits to iconic landmarks such as Tiger Hill, the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, and Ghoom Monastery.

Top Attractions in Darjeeling

  • Tiger Hill: Famous for its sunrise view over the Kanchenjunga range.
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, also known as the Toy Train.
  • Ghoom Monastery: A serene Tibetan Buddhist monastery, one of the oldest in Darjeeling.
  • Darjeeling Tea Plantations: Walk through lush tea estates and learn about the tea-making process.

Why Visit Darjeeling?

Darjeeling is an enchanting hill station known for its cool climate, sprawling tea estates, and captivating Himalayan views. The famous Toy Train ride, along with visits to monasteries and cultural spots, make it a top destination for nature lovers, adventurers, and peace seekers alike.

Package Highlights

  • Enjoy the Toy Train ride through scenic landscapes
  • Witness the sunrise at Tiger Hill
  • Visit tea plantations and learn about tea processing
  • Explore the cultural sites of Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop
  • Relax and take in the majestic views of the Himalayas

Description

Experience the wilderness with our Bangalore to Kabini wildlife safari package. This is a perfect getaway for wildlife enthusiasts, offering jeep safaris and boat rides through Kabini’s forests.

Top Attractions in Kabini wildlife safari

  • Elephant and Big Cat Spotting: Kabini is famous for its elephant herds, along with opportunities to spot leopards and tigers.
  • Kabini River Safaris: Boat safaris offer views of crocodiles, birds, and wildlife along the riverbanks.
  • Over 250 bird species, including migratory birds, make Kabini a bird lover’s paradise.
  • Nagarhole National Park: Jeep safaris provide access to rich biodiversity, including sightings of the rare black panther.
  • Nature Walks and Bird Watching: Experience serene nature walks with guided bird watching tours, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity of the Kabini area.

Why Visit Kabini Wildlife Sanctuary?

Kabini Wildlife Sanctuary offers a unique opportunity to connect with nature in its purest form. This lush green paradise is home to diverse wildlife, including majestic elephants, leopards, and numerous bird species. The tranquil river and picturesque landscapes create a perfect backdrop for adventure and relaxation. Whether you’re an avid wildlife photographer, a nature lover, or just looking for a peaceful escape, Kabini provides an unforgettable experience. Enjoy guided safaris that ensure a close encounter with wildlife while supporting conservation efforts in the region.

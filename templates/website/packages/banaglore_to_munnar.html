Description

Take a relaxing hill station trip with our Bangalore to Munnar package. Perfect for families and couples, this package includes a scenic drive and visits to tea gardens and waterfalls.

Top Attractions in Munnar Hill Station

  • Tea Gardens: Expansive tea plantations offering picturesque views and tea factory tours.
  • Eravikulam National Park: Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, with lush landscapes and trekking paths.
  • Mattupetty Dam: A popular spot for boating and scenic views of the surrounding hills.
  • Anamudi Peak: The highest peak in South India, perfect for adventurous treks and panoramic views.
  • Attukal Waterfalls: A beautiful cascade nestled between rolling hills, ideal for nature lovers.

Why Visit Munnar Hill Station?

Munnar is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers, with its misty mountains, lush greenery, and serene atmosphere. The cool climate, tea estates, and wildlife make it a perfect getaway for peace, relaxation, and exploration. Whether you're interested in trekking, wildlife, or just enjoying the scenic beauty, Munnar offers a refreshing break from city life.

Description

Escape to the cool weather and scenic beauty of Ooty, one of South India’s most famous hill stations. Our Bangalore to Ooty travel package offers a perfect combination of relaxation and exploration. Breathe in the fresh mountain air and visit beautiful gardens, lakes, and tea estates.

Top Attractions in Ooty

  • Ooty Lake: Enjoy boating on this beautiful lake surrounded by greenery.
  • Doddabetta Peak: The highest peak in the Nilgiri Hills, offering panoramic views of the surroundings.
  • Tea Gardens: Take a relaxing stroll through the sprawling tea estates and learn about tea production.
  • Botanical Gardens: A popular spot for nature lovers with a wide variety of plant species.

Why Visit Ooty?

Ooty, often referred to as the "Queen of Hill Stations," is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. With its pleasant climate, stunning landscapes, and vibrant flora, Ooty is perfect for a rejuvenating retreat. Experience the rich heritage of tea plantations and the tranquility of the hills while indulging in various outdoor activities like trekking and boating.
